   - Consider offering incentives or discounts to the client in the "Cloud Migration" opportunity to maintain their interest and commitment during this challenging period.

4. **Long-term Improvement Plan**:
   - Conduct a thorough review of the API integration processes to identify root causes of the authentication failures. This may involve updating documentation, improving training for clients, or enhancing the API's robustness.
   - Develop a customer success strategy that includes regular check-ins and support for clients to prevent similar issues in the future.

By addressing these insights and recommendations, Innovatek Solutions can mitigate risks, enhance client satisfaction, and position itself for future growth in the competitive high-tech landscape.

# Next Best Action Pattern (Compressed)

**Version**: 2.0 (Compressed for V2.0 Builder)
**Category**: Pattern

---

Every action: WHO does WHAT by WHEN and WHY?

**Requirements:**
- Specific: Use names, dates (not "stakeholder", "soon")
- Actionable: Clear verbs (Schedule, Send, Validate)
- Deadline: by Friday, within 48h, before [event]
- Evidence: Cite data (Task X overdue 7d)
- Priority: CRITICAL > HIGH > MEDIUM

**Triggers:** Overdue tasks, MEDDIC gaps, stakeholder gaps >14d

**Output:** Action | Why | Impact | Priority | Owner | Deadline | Success
